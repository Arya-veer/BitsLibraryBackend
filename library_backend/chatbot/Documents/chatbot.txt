Registration Steps

Please use the following Registration step for getting access to CAS Sci-Finder

Registration Steps for Creating Account:

Step 1: Create your login ID for SciFinder web version by clicking on link below:
https://scifinder.cas.org/registration/index.html?corpKey=D0A3609EX86F35055X3ED4517117449D5FD2

Step 2: You will receive confirmation email titled "SciFinder Registration - Your Confirmation Required" in few minutes. Please click on the link email (in 48 Hrs) and activate your access

Step 3: You can then visit https://scifinder.cas.org to use SciFinder web version!

** Password Reset : https://accounts.cas.org/password/

Registration Steps for Creating Account at CMIE databases :

How to Create User Name and Password to Access CMIE Database Services.  

Step 1: Visit the website http://register.cmie.com/ . You will be prompted to enter user name and password. 
Step 2: Click on "New User Registration" A registration form will appear. 
Step 3: Fill the form and click "Submit"  A system generated password will be sent to you on the registered email id. Use this UserID and Password to access the CMIE database services. 

How to register for Grammarly access

To register for the access, please follow the steps below:
Step 1: Go to https://www.grammarly.com/edu
Step 2: Click on Join Your Organization button, fill up the self-service form. Ensure that users are registering from their respective official domain email id.
Step 3: After clicking signup, an activation email will be sent to the user's institutional email ID.
